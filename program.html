About the Programme

Dance learning designed for preschoolers

The BeU FOUR-M KPOP Dance Programme is our signature preschool collaboration programme, specially designed for children aged 3–6.

Why BeU FOUR-M is Different?

Children don’t just copy dance moves, they learn how to understand movement.

Unlike typical KPOP dance classes that focus on teaching one new chorus in every session, the BeU FOUR-M KPOP Dance Programme is built around a structured preschool learning framework.

Each 4-class unit focuses on one song, allowing children to progressively build confidence and skill instead of rushing through choreography.

Through the programme, children will:

4-Class Learning Journey

Class 1

Musicality

Learn rhythm awareness, counting beats, and understanding musical timing.

Class 2

Movement

Develop movement fundamentals based on the choreography.

Class 3

Maturity

Practise, refine, and confidently complete the full chorus.

Class 4

Moment

Explore facial expressions, performance skills, and enjoy their special spotlight moment through performance or filming.

Why This Matters for Preschoolers?

Built around repetition, structure, and gradual progression.

Children aged 3–6 learn best through repetition, structure, and gradual progression.

Fast-paced “learn a new dance every week” formats can be overwhelming and often prioritise short-term copying over actual learning.

BeU FOUR-M is designed differently which is helping children truly absorb, enjoy, and grow.
